What is a Cyber Physical System (CPS)?

A CPS is basically a computer that controls sensors or motors. When computer controllers and physical systems are closely linked and rely on each other it's a CPS

Examples of CPS include smart grid, autonomous cars, medical devices, Industrial Control Systems (ICS) and robotics systems. You can find CPS in every day life. Including fairgrounds

However when we have computers and physical systems so closely linked it means that if something goes wrong with one it impacts the other...
